How To Use - 
1. Double click on "index.html" to open it in the browser.
2. Click on the browse button on the top left of the screen to select the song to annotate
3. Start the song with the play button
4. Press and hold the "l" key for the duration that the lead singer is singing and the "c" key for the chorus
    Note - 
    a) Mark only sections where there are vocals. 
    b) Instrumental tracks need not be annotated. 
    c) For songs where there is no chorus, mark all sections with vocals as lead.  
5. After annotating the entire song, copy the timestamps for lead and chorus into the "Lead Segments" 
and "Chorus Segments" columns of the excel sheet respectively
6. To annotate the next song, refresh the page and repeat
